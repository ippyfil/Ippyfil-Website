    Everything stared with the launch of a crowdfunding campaign with the goal to build a nano brewery equiped with a sofisticated electronically controlled 100 L HERMS. Exceeding every expectations, we outdo our goal begining this project.

    Ready to produce

    Ippyfil got ready to brew. The renovations process of the space was challenging making them woodworkers, electricians, painters, bricklayer, interior designers... a process that took some months. Now Ippyfil is exactly what was dream about, a brand representing hte dreams of both and the capacity of turning a empty space in a place full of life, beer and new challenges.
